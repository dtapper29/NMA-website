How to Identify a Mold Problem in Vinings, Kennesaw, Acworth, Woodstock & Throughout Metro Atlanta

A mold problem should never be ignored, especially when the toxic substance has invaded your living space. Some types of mold can cause health problems that can range in severity from coughing and skin irritation to central nervous system damage. Some types of cancer are even thought to come from mold exposure. In order to ensure you and your loved ones are protected from these health conditions, it is important to know the signs of mold infestation.

If you have a mold problem, your home is probably displaying one or more of the following signs:

  • Musty odors
  • Rusting or condensation around pipes, walls or windows
  • Peeling or cracked paint
  • Wood that has started to warp

All of these signs point toward excessive levels of moisture in your home, which can eventually lead to a mold problem and expose your loved ones to a variety of health risks associated with mold allergy. While some cases of mold infestation are undetectable, others are obvious. Mold growth can take on a variety of appearances, including white, pink, orange, green or black spots. Growth typically appears in bathroom tile, basements and other dark, humid spaces.
