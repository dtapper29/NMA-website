Black Mold Removal in Atlanta, Marietta, Alpharetta, Roswell, Smyrna, Sandy Springs & Beyond

The Centers for Disease Control and the Environmental Protection Agency recommend that black mold removal should only be attempted by a professional mold remediation company. In the Metro Atlanta area, you’ll find no professional mold removal company more attuned to the safety and convenience of its clients than NoMoldAtlanta. Owners Dan and JD Ortega have made it their mission to provide peace of mind for North Georgians concerned about the health risks of black mold, and the professional mold remediation services offered by NoMoldAtlanta are the proven safe, affordable and extremely effective for protecting the long-term health of you building’s inhabitants.

Ideally, black mold removal should be accomplished before symptoms like skin rash, nausea, mucous membrane irritation and others begin to cause issues for your family, pets or others who could come into contact with hidden mold in your home or place of business. If you suspect you may need professional mold removal, contact NoMoldAtlanta as soon as possible. Here’s what you can expect from the mold specialists from NoMoldAtlanta:

• When mold testing is required, we recommend testing by an independent third party, to ensure objectivity.
• Once unprotected areas are covered and the mold outbreak is isolated, the contaminated surfaces are cleaned by hand and our non-toxic, hydrogen peroxide-based Anabec Cleaning Solutions are applied to reduce existing mold.
• Then, our non-toxic Anasphere is applied to treated surfaces to deny mold spores their food source, preventing future colonies from developing. Our commercial and home mold removal process is backed by a limited, ten-year warranty.
• Because all our products are non-toxic, and because we practice far less “rip and tear” of existing building material, most house mold removal projects can be completed in 24-48 hours.
